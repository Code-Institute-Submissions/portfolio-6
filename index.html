About

I am a north London based Computer Science / Information Technology graduate. After graduating from the University of Hertfordshire, I was fortunate for having the experience of developing two main company websites for an exciting IT solutions company, Joskos Solutions, as a Marketing Executive Assistant. This developed my skillset within Online Marketing and Graphic Design outside my comfort zone. Alongside this I also worked as a Technician for Dixons Carphone for over three years due to my passion for technology. I also embarked on a short project for 360GSP, an IT training and recruitment company, as a Designer / Developer which solidified my skills using the latest industry standards.

Due to the nature of the Web Development industry which is continuously growing at an exponential rate, I wanted to ensure I have the relevant skills and knowledge to tackle real world scenarios within the industry. This is where Code Institute comes in. I am currently studying for a Diploma in Software Development through Code Institute in a course which is aligned and accredited by the Edinburgh Napier University. Upon completion, I will fulfil my goals of becoming a Full Stack Web Developer which will enable me to integrate both Front End and Back End systems seamlessly.
